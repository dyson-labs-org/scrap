Security Properties: No Trusted Third Parties

Cryptographic Guarantees for Space Operations

PropertyMechanism
AuthenticationX3DH mutual auth (no PSK required)
AuthorizationOperator-signed capability tokens
Forward SecrecyEphemeral keys deleted after session
AtomicityAdaptor signatures: complete or refund
Non-repudiationCryptographic proof of execution
LPI/LPDSession-derived spreading codes
WHAT AN ATTACKER CANNOT DO:
  • Forge capability tokens
  • Replay tokens (unique jti + cache)
  • Steal payments (timeout refund)
  • Eavesdrop on ISL (AES-256-GCM)
  • Impersonate satellites (X3DH)
