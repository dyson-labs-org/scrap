# SCAP/SISL Funding Opportunities

Last updated: 2025-12-29

This document tracks grant and contract opportunities relevant to SCAP (Satellite Capability and Payment Protocol) and SISL (Secure Inter-Satellite Link) development.

---

## Critical Notice: SBIR/STTR Program Status

The SBIR/STTR program authorization **expired September 30, 2025**. NASA, Space Force, and other agencies cannot release new SBIR/STTR solicitations nor fund new awards until Congress reauthorizes the program.

**Legislative Status:**
- H.R. 3169 (SBIR/STTR Reauthorization Act of 2025) introduced but not passed
- Ongoing awards remain valid
- Monitor for reauthorization before planning SBIR submissions

**Impact:** Space Force officials have publicly warned this is putting space acquisition timelines at risk.
